Ghost Rider The Avengers Deadpool
A motorcyclist was deceived by a devil from hell to sign a contract to sell his soul. That man has become a servant for the devil. His mission is to collect more souls in the world for the devil. However, that man after signing the contract also gained the power of an ancient demon known as the Ghost Rider. He became a flaming skull riding a burning motorbike. With this newly possessed power, he decided to betray the devil and get back his soul. The movie takes place in a fantasy world where superhero exists. An undercover organization named S.H.I.E.L.D had an idea of gathering a group of superhero together to work with each other and to defeat other bigger threats. Having been diagnosed with cancer, Wade Wilson decided to take part in an experiment in hope of curing himself. The experiment was successful, except that it completely destroyed his face. The leader of the group turned out to be a cruel guy who just wanted Wade to become his soldier. Having escaped from the experiment with a hideous face, Wade Wilson also possesses the power of unlimited regeneration. He decided to wear a superhero suit under the name of Deadpool and seek out his revenge
